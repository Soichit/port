ASSASSIN GAME ANDROID DEVELOPMENT

A location-based, multiplayer game called Assassin (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Assassin_ (game)) for mobile devices. For those unfamiliar with the game, Assassin, every participating player will be randomly assigned a target to kill, creating a “kill-chain”. If a player is able to kill their target, they will move onto their next target in their “kill-chain” and keep killing their targets until they are either eliminated themselves or there are 2 remaining players. The last 2 remaining players will fight in a sudden death to see who can approach their opponent faster and hit the “kill” button.

What I learned:

  • Android
  • LinkedLists

PUZZLE GAME FRONT END DEVELOPMENT

The Fifteen Puzzle (also called the Sliding Puzzle) is a simple classic game consisting of a 4x4 grid of numbered squares with one square missing. The object of the game is to arrange the tiles into numerical order by repeatedly sliding a square that neighbors the missing square into its empty space. This was a great project for me to practice using JavaScript to manipulate the DOM elements.

What I learned:

  • JavaScript
